Vision

AI-assisted development dramatically increases the speed at which code, 
experiments, and architectural changes can be produced. As AI agents begin 
to participate directly in the development process, the primary challenge 
shifts from writing code to coordinating work.

ForgeOps exists to ensure that accelerated development remains organized, 
visible, and controllable.

The long-term vision for ForgeOps is to provide a unified operational view 
of work across the entire AI development ecosystem. Instead of relying on 
memory, scattered issue trackers, or informal notes, ForgeOps maintains a 
clear ledger of work that spans repositories, agents, and development 
sessions.

In this vision, every task has a defined state, a responsible executor, 
and a recorded outcome. Work progresses through a clear lifecycle—from 
creation, to assignment, to execution, to review, and finally to closure. 
This lifecycle provides the structure necessary for both human developers 
and AI agents to collaborate effectively.

ForgeOps also enables parallel workflows where AI agents continue executing 
new tasks while previous work is reviewed by humans. By maintaining a 
reliable record of assignments, progress, and outcomes, ForgeOps ensures 
that development can continue without losing clarity or context.

As the ecosystem evolves, ForgeOps becomes the operational control board 
for the system. It provides a cross-repository view of progress, highlights 
blocked or stalled work, and ensures that the state of development remains 
legible even as the scale and speed of AI-assisted work increase.

The ultimate goal is a development environment where ideas move smoothly 
from planning to execution while maintaining full visibility into what 
work exists, what has been completed, and what should happen next.

ForgeOps makes this possible by turning AI-driven development from a 
collection of isolated activities into a coherent, traceable process.
